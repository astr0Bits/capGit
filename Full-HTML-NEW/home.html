Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about SkillSwap

How do I get started on SkillSwap?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, create your profile highlighting your skills and interests, and start browsing available skills or offering your own expertise to the community.

Is SkillSwap completely free to use?

Yes! SkillSwap is completely free for basic usage. We operate on a credit system where you earn credits by teaching others and spend credits to learn new skills. Premium features are available for those who want additional benefits.

How does the skill credit system work?

When you teach a skill, you earn credits based on the session duration and complexity. You can then use these credits to learn other skills from community members. This creates a balanced ecosystem of giving and receiving knowledge.

Can I use SkillSwap for professional development?

Absolutely! Many professionals use SkillSwap to enhance their skills, network with others in their industry, and even find mentorship opportunities. We offer specialized categories for business and professional skills.
